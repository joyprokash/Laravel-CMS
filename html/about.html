Corporate History

Micromax General Trading LLC is a global manufacturing company of electronics and satellite equipments. With discipline and ethics, MICROMAX possesses a thorough understanding of the global electronics and satellite market gained through our business experience since 2005.

Micromax General Trading LLC, established in 2005 has earned a reputation of the most trusted and sophisticated electronics, satellite and home appliances products in the world at competitive pricing with the added benefit of a customer friendly environment.

We strive to provide the latest technology and luxury brands at affordable prices to all our customers. Our core competency is our customer relationship. We have built a trust with our everyday consumer and overseas clients, which has provided us with regular business for over one decade.

The brand Micromax is now the most trusted brand in the Middle-East, Africa and other Asian countries such as Malaysia, Pakistan, Bangladesh, etc. We are manufacturer of electronics, mobile communications, home appliances, office equipment, and luxury goods such as TV, Dish Antenna, Rice cooker, Torch light, satellite receiver and Iron etc.

Our target market ranges from all age groups to all genders. We also manufacture various brands of TV, home appliances, satellite equipments and electronics for day-to-day convenience.

We strongly believe in great customer service and work towards the same. In doing so, we offer extended warranties and special offers to our customers.
